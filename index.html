How BLE Works in Retail Management

Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) is used in retail stores to connect with customers' smartphones through small devices called beacons.

How it Works:

  1. BLE beacons are installed inside the store.
  2. Beacons send Bluetooth signals with a unique ID.
  3. Customerâ€™s phone detects the beacon signal (if Bluetooth & store app are ON).
  4. The store app receives the signal and identifies the location or product area.
  5. Customer receives notifications like offers or product info.

Uses in Retail:

  • Personalized discount messages
  • Indoor navigation (like Google Maps inside store)
  • Fast and contactless billing
  • Tracking customer movement patterns
  • Product information pop-ups

Example:

If a customer comes near the shoe section, the store app may show: "20% OFF on Nike Shoes Today!"
